How it works!



Prowess is intended for use by prepaid service providers like internet reseller and satellite distributors. Prowess is designed to help you organize and manage collection of monthly subscriptions and monitor the process live via the internet.

Prowess Make-up and Features:

Prowess is a web and mobile application. The “First Payment” feature from Prowess allows the user to determine the amount to be collected from a newly connected client. Prowess “Reports” provide the Administrator with a live update of paid and unpaid invoices as well as the “Transaction Time” and the “Employee” in charge. Prowess allows you to upload data pertaining to all your clients through its “Import Data” feature thereby avoiding tedious manual entry.

How Prowess Works:

First you “Register” the employee names (users, supervisors, managers & admins), the “Service Plans” you offer and the “Locations” where you distribute your service. Then you upload your client data using the “Import Data” feature.
Using the mobile app, a “User” (Collection Officer) would be able to see a “Schedule” of clients with due invoices that he can visit for collection. Every time a collection is made, it is simultaneously recorded on the web for the Admin to see.
In case of a new client, the “User”, through the mobile app can create a new client account and charge the new client for a corresponding “First Payment”. Again, all of this is instantaneously recorded on the web for the Admin to monitor.
Every beginning of the month (or payment due date), new invoices are automatically generated for all “Active” clients; ready for collection.
