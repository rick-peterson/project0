Overview



Prowess is a program and mobile application for the collection of monthly invoices. Prowess is designed to serve small cable and internet service providers.

Prowess allows its users to:

  • Monitor bill collecting
  • Get live data while collecting
  • Save time organizing your receipts and records
  • Receive instant report for “Paid” and “Non-paid” invoices
  • Replace traditional invoicing with a printed receipt using a portable printer
  • Secure and instant archiving of all processes
